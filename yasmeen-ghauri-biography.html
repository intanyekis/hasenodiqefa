Who is Yasmeen Ghauri ?

Yasmeen Ghauri is a former Canadian supermodel. She is one of the Canadian fashion icons who has been credited with upbringing the change in the mentality of the local fashion industry.

In the early and mid-1990s, she walked on ramps across cities such as Paris, London, New York, and Milan for acclaimed fashion designers.

Yasmeen Ghauri: Age, Parent, Ethnicity

Yasmeen Ghauri was born on 23 March 1971, in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. She grew up in Montreal with her parents.

She is 50 years old. Her father, Moin Ghauri is Pakistani and her mother, Linda Ghauri is German.

She was born into a Muslim family. Ghauri does not have any siblings. She and her parents went for the Islamic Hajj pilgrimage to Mecca at the age of 12.

She holds Canadian nationality and is of German-Asian ethnicity.

In which school did Yasmeen Ghauri attend?

Yasmeen Ghauri: Career, Professional life

Yasmeen Ghauri was just at the age of 17 when she took the step to fulfill her childhood dream of being a model.

She was working at a McDonald’s restaurant and was discovered by Edward Zaccaria, a Montreal hairdresser, and Platine Coiffure artistic director.

Yasmin gets the chance like that and starts her career. Her parents didn’t approve of her though she pursued her career as a model.

Slowly, Yasmeen Ghauri’s career shifted to the fashion industry. In the year 1990, she was premiered in Milan and Paris. Then she moved to New York.

Then Yasmeen was featured in a music video for Elton John‘s song Sacrifice, along with Chris Isaak.

Yasmeen Ghauri was also seen on the cover of French Elle magazine. Furthermore, she also appeared on the cover of Italian Vogue magazine and in British and Italian Vogue editorials.

This supermodel also worked with both Chanel and Jil Sander.

Likewise, she worked with various fashion companies such as Chanel, Jean-Paul Gaultier, Helmut Lang, and Lanvin.

In 1991, Yasmeen Ghauri became the face of Anne Klein and Christian Dior. Following year, she had a contract with Victoria’s Secret, becoming the face of Valentino couture and Versace. She walked the annual Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show in 1996.

Ghauri retired by stepping out of the Yves Saint Laurent show in 1996 at the last minute and is now settled with her family.
