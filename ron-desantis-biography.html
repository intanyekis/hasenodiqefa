Who is Ron Desantis?

Ronald DeSantis is a known American politician and lawyer. He serves as the 46th ‘Republican’ governor of the U.S. state of Florida since 2019.

At the U.S. House of Representatives in the middle district of Florida from 2013 to 2018 he also served as a federal prosecutor.
