These Celebs Are Turning 60 This Year

June 2024 · 5 minute read

It’s no secret that in the world of the rich and famous age is definitely important — and the younger one is the more likely they are to be at the peak of their fame. However, there are some celebs who manage to stay famous even when they're older and today's list takes a look at those stars who are turning 60 in 2021.

Related: 10 Celebs Who Are Turning 50 In 2021

Yes — 60 is definitely considered old in the world of showbiz but these stars certainly prove that age is nothing but a number as they continue on with their success stories.

10 George Clooney Turns 60 On May 6, 2021

Kicking the list off is Hollywood star George Clooney who was born on May 6, 1961. The actor rose to fame as Dr. Doug Ross on the medical drama ER in the 90s and he has been a staple in the industry ever since. George — who admitted that he has been cutting his own hair for 25 years — has starred in plenty of award-winning movies some of them being Up in the Air and The Descendants.

9 Barack Obama Turns 60 On August 4, 2021

Let’s move on to former US President Barack Obama who was born on August 4, 1961. The politician — who was in office from January 2019 until January 2017 — has definitely managed to remain one of the most beloved former presidents and together with his wife Michelle Obama he still remains very active in the world in the world of politics!

8 Billy Ray Cyrus Turns 60 On August 25, 2021

Another male celebrity who is turning 60 this year is Billy Ray Cyrus. The singer and songwriter was born on August 25, 1961, and he rose to fame in the 90s with his hit single "Achy Breaky Heart".

Related: 10 Celebs Who Are Turning 40 In 2021

To a younger audience, Billy got introduced as Miley Cyrus’s father as he starred alongside his daughter on the Disney Channel show Hannah Montana. Into 2019 Billy was once again at the peak of his fame as he was featured on a remix of Lil Nas X's song "Old Town Road".

7 Heather Locklear Turns 60 On September 25, 2021

The first lady on today’s list is Heather Locklear who was born on September 25, 1961. Heather is best known for her role as Sammy Jo Carrington on Dynasty in the 80s as well as her role as Amanda Woodward on Melrose Place in the 90s. While Heather stuck to television for most of her career, some of her more notable big-screen roles include Firestarter, Money Talks, and The Perfect Man.

6 Eddie Murphy Turns 60 On April 3, 2021

Next on the list is Hollywood star Eddie Murphy who was born on April 3, 1961. Eddie — who is also a comedian, writer, and singer — is best known for his performances in the show Beverly Hills Cop which he joined in the 80s as well as blockbusters such as Trading Places, The Nutty Professor, Dr. Dolittle, Coming to America, and Dolemite Is My Name.

5 Carole Baskin Turns 60 On June 6, 2021

Big-cat rights activist and reality television star Carole Baskin has also made it onto today’s list. Carole was born on June 6, 1961, and she rose to international fame in the 2020 Netflix documentary show Tiger King. Carole — who is a huge lover of cats — will certainly continue fighting for their well-being even once she turns 60 later this year.

4 Julia Louis-Dreyfus Turned 60 On January 13, 2021

Let’s move on to yet another famous actress — this time we're talking about Julia Louis-Dreyfus. Julia — who was born on January 13, 1961 — is best known for her participation in shows such as Saturday Night Live, Seinfeld, The New Adventures of Old Christine, and Veep.

Besides being a big name on television, Julia — who also has a very sucessful family —has also stared in quite a few famous movies like Hannah and Her Sisters, National Lampoon's Christmas Vacation, and Deconstructing Harry.

3 Meg Ryan Turns 60 On November 19, 2021

Another famous actress that has made it onto today’s list is Meg Ryan. Meg — who was born on November 19, 1961 — rose to fame in the 80s with her role on the soap opera As the World Turns as well as roles in movies such as Top Gun, Promised Land, and When Harry Met Sally... Since the 80s, Meg has been a staple in the movie industry and she has started in plenty of blockbusters some of which are Sleepless in Seattle, Courage UnderFire, and Proof of Life.

2 Woody Harrelson Turns 60 On July 23, 2021

Let’s move on to Hollywood star Woody Harrelson who was born on July 23, 1961. The actor — who is a recipient of numerous awards — is probably best known for starring in movies such as The People vs. Larry Flynt, The Messenger, and, Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri, as well as the first season of the HBO crime show True Detective. Over the years Woody became close with plenty of other stars in the industry — most notably with actor Matthew McConaughey.

1 Adrienne Maloof Turns 60 On September 4, 2021

Wrapping the list up is reality television star and socialite Adrienne Maloof who was born on September 4, 1961. The diva rose to fame as a cast member of Bravo's popular reality television show The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills and even though she officially left after season three, she has made guest appearances throughout later seasons, so who knows — perhaps fans of the show will get to see more of Adrienne as a 60-year-old!
