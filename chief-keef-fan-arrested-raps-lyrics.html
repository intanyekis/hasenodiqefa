Chief Keef Fan Recites Rappers Lyrics While Being Arrested

June 2024 · 2 minute read

A Chief Keef fan who was in the process of getting arrested called upon some fitting lyrics from the Chicago rhymer to hilariously narrate the moment.

The February arrest recently went viral after a bystander posted it to TikTok and Instagram. In the clip, a man who appears to go by the name Lil B (not the famous Bay Area rapper) started rapping Keef’s 2014 track “Earned It” as two officers are putting him in handcuffs – letting them know he had bail money on deck.

“I’ma bond right out I ain’t broke (I ain’t broke)/ N-ggas ain’t getting no money it ain’t a joke!” Lil B raps in the clip.

The officers found it as amusing as the bystanders did and laughed along with everyone, even as B got in their faces to let them know he was actually out on bond from a different situation. “And I’m out on bond! Fuck you talmbout?” he said. “And I’m out on bond and I just out yesterday!”

Lil B isn’t the only Chief Keef fan who can spit his lyrics on a dime. GloRilla has been loud and proud about her love for Sosa for some time now and not too long ago rapped one of his songs while talking to HipHopDX on a red carpet.

“She say she love me, whatever that is/ Do me a favor, save that shit,” she rapped with a smile on her face at the 2022 American Music Awards.
