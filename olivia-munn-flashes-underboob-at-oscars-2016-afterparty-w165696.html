Olivia Munn Flashes Underboob at Oscars 2016 Afterparty

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Flashy! Olivia Munn put her toned body on display at the 2016 Vanity Fair Oscar afterparty — and even flashed a little underboob.

The X-Men: Apocalypse actress, 35, wore a beaded gunmetal gown by J. Mendel that featured a thigh-high slit and cutouts near the rib cage. She accessorized the glitzy number with diamond earrings and black strappy heels.

Munn’s second dress of the night was a marked difference from her first frock, an orange column gown by Stella McCartney, which kept her covered from head to toe with the exception of one shoulder. She paired that ensemble with a Jimmy Choo clutch and stud earrings and a cuff (both by Forevermark) that added a subtle sparkle to the outfit. 

The Mortdecai actress was arm in arm with her boyfriend, Green Bay Packers quarterback Aaron Rodgers, for both occasions, and even snapped a fun pic during the show of the two eating snacks in the audience. “Girl scout cookies FTW!!” she wrote via Instagram.

Cookies might have been Munn’s food of choice at the Oscars, but she recently claimed that Japanese potatoes are responsible for her glowing skin. “I’ve talked about this before and I still stand by it: Japanese potatoes that are high in Hyaluronic Acid help keep wrinkles away,” the Newsroom alum shared on Instagram. “Look up this video: ‘Connie Chung Yuzihara’ to learn all about it. There are Hyaluronic Acid pills and vitamins but I think that the best way to get it in your system is by eating them in foods that naturally have them.”
