When Tim Burton's fantasy horror comedy film Beetlejuice hit theaters in 1988, it was an instant cult classic.

Its hilarious blend of the macabre and the mundane, along with stellar performances, made it a box office smash, grossing over $74 million against a budget of just $15 million.

The film spawned an exciting animated series, video games, and even a stage musical, ensuring its place in popular culture.

With Beetlejuice 2 (Beetlejuice Beetlejuice) set to hit the theaters in the summer, fans are buzzing about the return of stars Michael Keaton, Winona Ryder, and Catherine O'Hara, as well as intriguing new additions like Jenna Ortega, Justin Theroux, Monica Bellucci, and Willem Dafoe.

But as we revisit the strange and unusual world of Beetlejuice, let's dive into a more earthly question: just how much is the cast of Beetlejuice 2 worth?

6 Catherine O'Hara

Catherine O'Hara's Net Worth (Estimated) - $10 Million

A luminary of Canadian comedy, Catherine O'Hara rose to fame in the 1970s as a member of the legendary Second City improv troupe.

She first achieved recognition for her comedy work on Second City Television, and is currently associated with the breakout comedy Schitt's Creek.

O'Hara's resume is filled with comedy dramas like Martin Scorsese's After Hours, and her scene-stealing role as Delia Deetz in Beetlejuice. But it was her role as Kate McCallister in the Home Alone franchise that landed her in the global spotlight.

The first two films in the series, Home Alone and Home Alone 2: Lost in New York, grossed $476.7 million and $359 million, respectively, at the box office.

Over the course of four decades in the industry, O'Hara has garnered an estimated net worth of $10 million. O'Hara has served as a voice artist in a number of animated films, like Frankenweenie and The Addams Family.

Additionally, she has ventured into writing and served as a voice artist for the upcoming movie The Wild Robot.

In the upcoming sequel, Beetlejuice 2, O'Hara will reprise her role as Delia Deetz.

5 Winona Ryder

Winona Ryder's Net Worth (Estimated) - $18 Million

Winona Ryder burst onto the Hollywood scene in the late 1980s with her debut in Lucas, but it was her role in Tim Burton's Beetlejuice that catapulted her to fame.

Quickly becoming a darling of the indie film scene, she starred in cult classics like Heathers, and partnered with Tim Burton again for Edward Scissorhands.

Ryder enjoyed critical acclaim for roles in The Age Of Innocence and Little Women, earning Oscar nominations. Despite a brief decline in her career following the release of Mr. Deeds, Ryder staged a comeback with her role in Star Trek.

Ryder has featured in over 40 films, all of which have collectively grossed over $2 billion at the box office.

Since 2016, she has starred as Joyce Byers in the Netflix science-fiction horror series Stranger Things, for which she received a Golden Globe nomination.

Ryder's involvement in Stranger Things helped her to a paycheck of $9.5 million, after it was bumped up from $2.8 million for the third season.

Ryder will return for the sequel of Beetlejuice in 2024 as Lydia Deetz, the tritagonist of the first film.

4 Michael Keaton

Michael Keaton's Net Worth (Estimated) - $40 Million

You can determine a person's generation by how they know Michael Keaton, whether through his comedic roles in the 1980s, his memorable portrayal of Batman, or his time as Vulture in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).

Keaton has been around the block for over four decades, and his films have earned more than $6 billion cumulatively. Keaton has an estimated net worth of $50 million.

He earned early recognition through his roles in Night Shift, Mr. Mom, and Beetlejuice. He gained wider stardom portraying the title superhero in Batman and Batman Returns, earning paychecks of $5 million and $11 million, respectively.

Over the years, Keaton has starred in a multitude of films, including Pacific Heights,Cars,Toy Story 3, and Minions. However, it was his role in Birdman that marked a career resurgence.

Keaton returned to the superhero genre, portraying the Vulture in the MCU's Spider-Man: Homecoming and reprising his role as Batman in The Flash in 2023, reminding audiences of his inimitable on-screen presence.

Keaton will be reprising his role as the titular character Beetlejuice for the sequel Beetlejuice 2, a role which is bound to further draw his fans to theaters through nostalgia.

3 Willem Dafoe

Willem Dafoe's Net Worth (Estimated) - $40 Million

A virtuoso of intensity and range, Willem Dafoe has played every role you can imagine.

His career in film kicked off in experimental theater, before he started appearing on the big screen in the early 1980s.

Dafoe has gained admiration for his diverse filmography, exceeding 100 films that have collectively raked in over $12 billion worldwide.

Willem Dafoe has an estimated net worth of $40 million, which reflects his enduring success in the industry.

His early breakthrough came with intense performances in films like Platoon, which grossed over $137 million worldwide.

While his sinister depiction of the Green Goblin in Sam Raimi's Spider-Man trilogy brought him mainstream recognition, Dafoe has consistently defied typecasting.

He continued to show his range with nuanced roles in independent gems like The Florida Project and The Lighthouse, and then returned to the blockbuster territory as Nuidis Vulko in the DC Comics films Aquaman and Zack Snyder's Justice League.

In 2023, Dafoe was announced as part of the cast in Beetlejuice 2 in an unknown role, but with Dafoe on board, moviegoers are sure to be entertained.

2 Justin Theroux

Justin Theroux's Net Worth (Estimated) - $40 Million

Justin Theroux is a multifaceted talent in Hollywood, and gained recognition for his collaborations with auteur director David Lynch in the films Mulholland Drive and Inland Empire.

He broke through with appearances in movies like American Psycho and Charlie's Angels: Full Throttle, the latter grossing over $259 million worldwide.

Theroux established himself as a screenwriter with contributions to blockbusters like the action comedy Tropic Thunder, the superhero blockbuster Iron Man 2, and the musical comedy-drama Rock of Ages.

While frequently appearing in strong supporting roles, he took center stage with his critically acclaimed performance as Kevin Garvey in HBO's The Leftovers.

Theroux has starred in over 25 films with a combined box office gross surpassing $2.8 billion.

Theroux also featured in other blockbusters such as Star Wars: The Last Jedi and Bumblebee; both movies earned $1.33 billion and $465 million, respectively, at the box office.

With a reported net worth of $40 million, Theroux's multifaceted career includes directorial ventures, such as his debut film Dedication, which grossed over $250,000.

1 Monica Bellucci

Monica Bellucci's Net Worth (Estimated) - $45 Million

Monica Bellucci is an international icon of beauty and cinematic presence.

She began her career as a highly sought-after fashion model, gracing campaigns for Dolce & Gabbana and other luxury brands.

She has played starring roles, supporting roles, and guest appearances within an eclectic filmography spanning a range of genres and countries, all of which have collectively grossed $3.4 billion worldwide.

Bellucci boasts a net worth of $45 million, stemming in part from her tenure as an Elite model and her prominent roles in Dolce & Gabbana campaigns.

Bellucci made her acting debut in the Italian television miniseries Vita Coi Figli, before gaining recognition as one of Dracula's brides in Bram Stoker's Dracula.

Her breakthrough came with the arthouse film The Apartment, leading to her introduction to American audiences in Under Suspicion in 2002.

She portrayed Persephone in the 2003 science-fiction sequels The Matrix Reloaded and The Matrix Revolutions, both of which earned $738 million and $427 million respectively.

Bellucci appeared in the James Bond film Spectre as Bond Girl; the film is her highest-grossing film, with a global box office return of $880 million.

Bellucci remains active in the fashion industry, serving as an ambassador for luxury brands like Cartier and Dior, and has the highest estimated net worth among the cast of Beetlejuice 2.
