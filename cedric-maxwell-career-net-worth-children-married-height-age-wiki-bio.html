Who is Cedric Maxwell?

Cedric Maxwell is an American retired professional basketball player, who is currently working in radio broadcasting. He was a star forward/center for the UNC Charlotte 49ers. Cedric was the 12th overall pick in the 1977 NBA draft.

Nicknamed as Cornbread, Cedric was born on November 21, 1955, in Kinston, North Carolina, USA. He joined Kinston High School and later enrolled at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte.
