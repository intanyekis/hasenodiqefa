Brooke Henderson's parents are Dave and Darlene Henderson. Her parents have supported her and her sister in pursuing their careers in golf, so they can be accredited for their daughter's success.

In this article, let's find out about the star Canadian golfer and her family information.

Brooke is among the prominent professional golfer in Canada on the LPGA tour. She is the recipient of Canadian Press female athlete of the year in 2015, 2017, and 2018. Currently, she has the most victories of any Canadian golfer on major professional tours.

From a young age, she was into golf and started to learn at the Smiths Falls Golf and Country Club. As she grew up, her fixation on golf bolstered and she started to perform extraordinarily, winning her first major when the golfer was just 18 years old.

LPGA Brooke Henderson Parents: Dave Henderson And Darlene Henderson

Dave and Darlene are the proud parents of Brooke Henderson. They both are experienced golf players themselves, so it can be said that the inspiration for her to pursue golf came from them.

Her parents managed the environment so that she could easily access golf learning. She was enrolled in the Smiths Falls Golf and Country Club early because of her and her parents' interest in sports.

Her parents are very proud of their daughter's achievements and can be seen talking about it. There is an interview of them published by LGPA where they talk about her and her sister's golf journey.

They said that the family has a cottage around 40 minutes from their hometown, and a nine-hole course called Rideau Lakes Golf and Country Club is located where Brooke used to practice.

Brooke Henderson Family & Siblings

Brooke's family consist of her parents and sibling. Her sibling's name is Brittany Henderson, who is also a golf player like her; however, currently, she can be seen with Brooke as her caddie.

The sisters are very close to each other, and usually, they can be seen together on the court in Brooke's competitions. Brooke has said that her sister's interest in golf is also why she is a penchant for sports.

Brittany was a top junior and college golfer. She has been a role model to her sister and has been very supportive. It can be said that the entire Henderson family is very much into golf.

For many years, Brooke's uncle, Tom Henderson, was seen competing at the top amateur level in the Ottawa area. Brittany is also very private, and only limited information about her life is available to the public.
