The creator of American Horror Story, Ryan Murphy, bought this haunting mystery on Netflix. The first season premiered on October 13th, 2022, with a supremely talented cast. Bobby Cannavale, Naomi Watts, Mia Farrow, Jennifer Coolidge, and Isabelle Gravitt gave stellar performances in the miniseries. The Watcher has received rave reviews from the audience and critics; it was trending on Netflix at the number one spot for weeks. Due to its instant popularity, the series has been renewed for a second season.

The protagonists' names have been changed in the series from the original victims to protect their identities. All the characters are inspired by their real counterparts.

Isabel Gravitt ($500-650,000)

The 19-year-old actress is well known for her role in Little Fires Everywhere (2021). She has established herself with a net worth of $500-650,000k through her acting career. Isabel has also appeared in American Housewife (2016) and Cucuy: The Boogeyman (2018). She is currently filming Flycatcher, a crime-based film.

The Watcher has garnered worldwide attention for its twisted horror tale and memorable characters based on real life. It will be interesting to see the stellar cast once again reprise their respective roles to thrill the audience in season two.

Luke David Blumm ($1.5 Million)

The Beyond the Rush actor plays Carter in The Watcher. His impressive filmography at a young age includes Daredevil (2015), The Walking Dead (2019), and Watchmen (2009). He recently appeared alongside comedian Pete Davidson in King of Staten Island (2021). The 13-year-old actor boasts of an estimated wealth of $1.5 million.

Margo Martindale ($4 Million)

The Emmy Award-winning actress is known for her roles in The Americans and Justified. According to CelebNetWorth, she has done voice-over for a Netflix comedy based on herself titled BoJack Horseman, which terms an additional means of having a net worth of $4 million. Apart from playing Maureen in the series, she is also known for Dead Men Walking and The Hours. Nominated for Tony Award in the play Cat on a Hot Tin Roof, the 74-year-old actress has gained popularity with her challenging roles in television.

Terry Kinney ($4 Million)

The Oz actor has essayed the role of Jasper in the hit Netflix series. His estimated wealth of $4 million mirrors his success as a director, technical consultant, and co-founder of Steppenwolf Theatre Company. His passion remains in starring and directing stage plays. The actor has recently appeared in the crime drama The Little Things (2021) as Captain Farris.

Christopher McDonald ($4 Million)

Playing the suave detective Rourke Chamberland in The Watcher, Christopher is best known for his role in the hit film Grease 2 (1982). His estimated wealth of $4 million comes from his vast experience in theater, films, and television. He has starred in cult classics like Thelma & Louise (1991), Happy Gilmore (1996), and Requiem for a Dream (2000). In television, the actor has appeared in the famed Family Law (1999-2002) and been a voice-over artist for Costume Quest (2019), Batman Beyond (2000), and Kim Possible (2004).

Richard Kind ($5 Million)

The multi-talented actor is known for his impressive line of work in Stargate (1994), Mad About You (1992-1999), Spin City (1996-2002), A Bug’s Life (1998), Cars (2006-2011), Toy Story 3 (2010), and animated series on Netflix Big Mouth (2017-present). Kind is famous as a voice-over artist for Disney characters. He was nominated for the Screen Actors Guild Award for his role in Mad About You. In 2010 he won the Independent Spirit Award for his performance in A Serious Man. The actor essaying the role of Mitch in the hit Netflix series has accumulated $5 million.

Jennifer Coolidge ($6 Million)

Playing Karen in the thriller series is just one of the notable roles the American Pie actress has added to her impressive list of works. According to Parade, the scene-stealing characters played in Legally Blonde (2001- 2003) franchise were her claim to fame. Recently Jennifer has won accolades and a PrimeTime Emmy Award for the critically acclaimed HBO series The White Lotus. Her success has resulted in a $6 million net worth career.

Bobby Cannavale ($10 Million)

The Primetime Emmy Award winner has essayed the role of Dean Brannock in The Watcher series. He started his career with thrillers like Night Falls On Manhattan (1996) and The Bone Collector (1999). A recurring cast member on the hit sitcom Will & Grace (1998), he became a household name with his acting prowess. In 2008 he received a Tony nomination for his acclaimed role in the Broadway play Mauritius. With his expansive film, stage, and television career, Cannavale’s wealth is estimated at around $10 million.

Naomi Watts ($35 Million)

The Oscar-nominated actress plays the role of Nora Brannock in the thriller series. According to CelebrityNetWorth, her net worth is estimated at $35 million. She rose to stardom with King Kong (2005) and later continued the successful streak with The Impossible (2012) and the Divergent trilogy (2014-2016). Naomi has also starred in the mystery TV series Twin Peaks (2017). Recently she has forayed into the health and wellness industry by launching her own brand Stripes for menopausal women.

Mia Farrow ($60 Million)

Actor and director Mia have accumulated a net worth of $60 million. Apart from reprising the true character of Abby Langford as Pearl Winslow in the Netflix series, she has done acclaimed roles in Hollywood classics like Rosemary’s Baby (1968), The Great Gatsby (1974), and Death on the Nile (1978). Her theater work is equally impressive, being the first American actress to be inducted into the Royal Shakespeare Company. Jeanne d’Arc au Bucher, Peter Pan (1976), and Romantic Comedy (1979) are some memorable theatrical plays of the Golden Globe-nominated actress. She has also appeared in thirteen of fourteen Woody Allen films during the 80s, and from the 2000s to the present, her wealth is mainly generated from films, television, and stage productions.
